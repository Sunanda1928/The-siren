Problem :
Digital scams through SMS messages and phone calls are increasing rapidly.
Millions of people especially those in rural areas, elderly users, and non-English speakers are unable to identify whether a message is genuine or fraudulent.

Scam messages often:
1)Create fear or urgency
2)Ask users to click suspicious links
3)Request OTPs or personal details
4)Impersonate banks, government bodies, or service providers
5)Due to lack of awareness or language barriers, many users click these links and become victims of fraud.
